 1. Story: The Time-Traveling Pen
Emma found an old pen in her grandfather’s attic. When she wrote with it, she was transported to the year 1920, right in the middle of a bustling street where cars were still powered by steam.

Plot Twist: Emma realizes she’s not just visiting the past — she’s replacing her great-grandmother, who mysteriously vanished in 1920. If she doesn’t solve the mystery, she may never return.
