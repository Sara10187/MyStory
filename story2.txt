 2. Story: Max and the Talking Cat
Max was walking home when a stray cat looked at him and said, “Follow me if you want to save your future.” Confused but curious, Max followed the cat into an alley that glowed with a strange blue light.

Plot Twist: The cat is actually Max from the future, trapped in animal form by a time loop. Saving the future means Max has to break the loop — but it may erase his current memories.
