 3. Story: The Cave of Whispers
Deep in the forest, Lila discovered a cave where every whisper echoed with the voice of someone she had lost. One whisper stood out—it was calling her name and asking for help.

Plot Twist: The cave isn’t magical — it’s part of an advanced AI experiment. Lila’s memories were uploaded during her childhood, and now she must decide whether to stay in the digital world or return to reality.
